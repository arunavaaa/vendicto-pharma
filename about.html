Our Story

About Vendicto Pharma

A Nagpur-based pharmaceutical company built on the belief that quality medicine should be accessible — and that healthcare professionals deserve a partner they can rely on.

Nagpur, Maharashtra Ramdaspeth — Our Home Base
Who We Are

Built in Nagpur.
For Maharashtra's Health.

Vendicto Pharma Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a clear purpose: to make clinically sound, high-quality pharmaceutical products accessible to every corner of Maharashtra. Headquartered at Diwan Plaza in Ramdaspeth, Nagpur, we operate at the heart of one of India's key pharmaceutical markets.

We specialize in branded generic medicines — products that combine the scientific rigour of originator drugs with the accessibility of generic formulations. Our portfolio serves a wide range of therapeutic needs, spanning anti-inflammatory, anti-bacterial, respiratory, gastrointestinal, and nutritional categories.

Our customers are the healthcare professionals who trust us: doctors who prescribe with confidence, chemists who stock with certainty, and distributors who value predictable supply. Each product we bring to market is chosen for its clinical need, formulated for efficacy, and manufactured to quality standards that our partners can rely on.

At Vendicto, we believe that building long-term relationships — not just transactions — is what defines a great pharmaceutical partner. We are growing, and we are growing responsibly.
